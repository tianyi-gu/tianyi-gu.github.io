Welcome to Tianyi's Murder Mystery

Created for Ms. Robie's ENG515 Mystery Class

How to Play

  • You are a detective investigating the room of a potential murder suspect
  • You have 5 clues to help guide you around their house
  • Use Arrow Keys to move around the scene
  • Press Z to move up, C to move down
  • Click and drag to look around
  • Explore the room to see what you can learn about the person who lives here
  • Search for objects and what they imply about the person
  • Understand the person's personality and motivations
